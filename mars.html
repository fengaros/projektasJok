Deimos and Phobos:

Asaph Hall discovered Deimos on 12 August 1877 and Phobos at 18 August 1877. Phobos and Deimos compared to Luna(Earth moon) are really small, Phobos diameter is just 22.2 km, and Deimos only 12.6 km. [wiki]
